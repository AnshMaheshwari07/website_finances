Management Advisory Services

Our Goal is to be your business trusted advisors and more importantly, business partners. Our services can help improve your business performance whether your goal is to lower costs, improve efficiency or keep up with today’s rapidly changing technology. Talati & Talati LLP offers complete management advisory services, including systems implementation and management, cost controls, employee benefit plan restructuring, and much more.

In today’s continually changing and more highly competitive business environment, we believe that providing only accounting and tax services to our clients is insufficient to meet their growing needs. We have found that the ability to offer management advisory services is particularly important to new and emerging businesses which cannot routinely retain these skills on a full-time basis and to larger companies seeking the most cost effective and efficient operating methods.

Consequently, because of our ability to objectively view the entire range of business and financial operations within a company, we are well qualified to provide assistance in many areas of planning, operations improvement and decision making. We assist all types of companies – newly formed, well established or even troubled in many industries. Our professionals can be valuable during the different phases of the business cycle, assisting with problem solving and planning. Many companies have already seen benefits of working with us in their path to growth.
Clients can receive independent corporate mangement advice informed by our expert team in matters related to:

  • Gap Analysis of the existing performance under the Environmental, Social & Governance(ESG) parameters and benchmarking with Industry leaders.

  • Prioritization of key Stakeholders & Material Issues.

  • Transfer Pricing and all other services related to Income Tax and Direct Taxes.

  • Inclusion of ESG parameters in all systems & processes across the organization.

  • Develop a Roadmap for Sustainable growth with clear targets.

  • Sustainable Supply chain management with policies, review & monitoring mechanisms, ESG audits etc.

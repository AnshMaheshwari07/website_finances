Transaction Advisory Services

Our Transaction Services team helps clients identify risks in transactions, in order that they can make informed strategic decisions confidently. From deal negotiations to capturing synergies during integration, we help clients gain value and deliver it to stakeholders.

With professionals across Indian cities, Talati & Talati LLP is armed with experienced teams having deep industry and local market knowledge, as well as technical experience tailored to suit each client’s situation.

Transaction Advisory Services is unencumbered by audit independence concerns and offers seamless analytical service throughout the deal continuum. From decision support at origination to deal closing and beyond, we support our clients throughout every facet of the transaction. Our experts in the team provide due diligence based on finance, taxation, process management, information technology and cyber security to non-public equity, hedge funds and strategic investors.

We advise on all aspects of the investment cycle, employing an approach that focuses on value drivers and deal-breakers that are critical to investment decisions. We analyze the historical performance but also use this information to work out if the projections are supported reality. We inform our clients of the longer term benefits and pitfalls which will be encountered during a transaction.
At Talati & Talati LLP, the focus is on helping financial service clients to raise value from existing transactions through all stages of the deal life cycle. We offer Following Services in Transaction Advisory Services:
  • Mergers and Acquisition

  • Corporate Finance Strategy

  • Operational Transaction Advisory

  • Valuation & Business Modeling

  • Transaction Support Services

  • Deal Analytics and Closures

  • Business Due diligence
